Removal Request Guidelines

Lulbin will review content removal requests that claim a violation of our Terms of Service ("TOS"). For non-law enforcement agency requests, please contact us via Telegram at @lulbin.

Law Enforcement Request Guidelines

Lulbin will respond to all law enforcement requests from authorized officials. These Law Enforcement Request Guidelines outline how authorized law enforcement officials can engage with and contact us to request information or content removal in accordance with applicable laws and policies.

To facilitate processing, we require that all requests be addressed directly to Lulbin. Please include proper Mutual Legal Assistance Treaty documents if cross-border law enforcement is involved.

Lulbin does not retain any subscriber information that is not directly provided to us, such as IP addresses. We will only cooperate with data requests if accompanied by a valid court order, and will actively fight any attempt to subpoena or otherwise acquire any user information.

We will voluntarily cooperate with law enforcement requests from all jurisdictions for content removal requests when the content violates our TOS. All law enforcement requests should be submitted via email to the address provided above.
